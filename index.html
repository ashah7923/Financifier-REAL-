ABOUT THIS WEBSITE

Financifier allows users of all ages to effectively budget their finances. People with poor financial management skills may have difficulty purchasing a home, a car, or any other aspiration. Financifier assists users in identifying strategies that will enable them to achieve their objectives.

The primary goals of Financifier are centralized on the benefits of all that choose to use the new app.
  • We strive to enhance the experinces of new users through our attempts to educate the public on personal finance.
  • Another very important goal of ours is to help those in financial hardships manage their money. Specifically, this will help them understand how much money they should set aside for savings, pocket-spending, and essentials/taxes.
  • Lastly, due to our budget app's highly innovative features, any user can easily find success in a short period of time.
The app allows the user to receive instant financial advice, which leads to better overall money management. The app is designed to be useful/impactful to people of all ages, and we are competing with accountants who may be costly to hire. Financifier will be available at a low cost when it is released proving itself to be a cheaper alternative to hiring an accountant. For these various reasons, Financifier proves to be a cut above the rest.

In this section, input your monthly income so the app will automatically split that monthly income into three categories:

  • Needs
  • Wants
  • Savings

The significance of this calculator is that the app will easily calculate the "Needs" to be 50% of your monthly income, the "Wants" to be 30% of your monthly income, and the "Savings" to be 20% of your monthly income.

Needs: In this section, these are items that you must buy in order to live comfortably. For example, some needs include:

  • Rent
  • Food
  • Gas (for cars)
  • Basic Utilities
  • Insurance
  • Wi-fI

Wants: In this section, these are the items that you want to buy, especially if it is for entertainment purposes. Some wants include:

  • TV Subscriptions (i.e. Netflix)
  • Restaurant Meals
  • Video Games
  • Electronics
  • Vacations
  • Toys

Savings: This is the percentage of your monthly income where you would want to save and this will help you become financially afloat. You can save for:

  • Taking Away Debt
  • College Tuition For Your Children
  • Emergency Fund
  • Retirement Fund
